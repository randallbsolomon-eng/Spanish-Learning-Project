## 7. Common verbs (simple forms)

Present tense, *yo* (I) and *tú* (you informal) forms only.

| Spanish | English | Pronunciation |
|---------|---------|---------------|
| soy | I am (permanent) | *soy* |
| estoy | I am (location/state) | *es-TOY* |
| eres | you are (permanent) | *EH-res* |
| estás | you are (location/state) | *es-TAHS* |
| tengo | I have | *TEN-goh* |
| tienes | you have | *TYEH-nes* |
| voy | I go | *boy* |
| vas | you go | *bahs* |
| quiero | I want | *KYEH-roh* |
| quieres | you want | *KYEH-res* |
| como | I eat | *KOH-moh* |
| bebo | I drink | *BEH-boh* |
| hablo | I speak | *AH-bloh* |
| entiendo | I understand | *en-TYEN-doh* |

**Quick tip:** *Ser* (soy/eres) = identity, origin, time. *Estar* (estoy/estás) = location, mood, temporary state.
